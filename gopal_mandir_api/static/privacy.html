Privacy Policy

Shri Gopal Mandir — Digital Darshan & Seva App
Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Shri Gopal Mandir mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide while using specific features:

  • Account / membership: your mobile phone number, used to send a one-time password (OTP) for login and to identify your membership.
  • Forms and requests: your name, phone number, and email address when you submit a volunteer request, learning-program registration, astrology consultation request, community post, or feedback.
  • User content: comments, questions, and posts you submit to community and gallery sections, which may be visible to other users and temple staff.
  • Technical data: basic, non-identifying request information (such as device type and app version) needed to operate and secure the service.

The App does not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers, and it does not use third-party advertising or analytics tracking SDKs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide membership features.
  • To respond to your requests (volunteering, learning, consultations, feedback).
  • To display community content you choose to post.
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and proper functioning of the service.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information is accessible only to authorized temple staff and to service providers that host our infrastructure (for example, our cloud database and storage providers) strictly to operate the App. We may disclose information if required by law.

OTP / SMS

Phone numbers are used to send a one-time login code. Standard carrier message rates may apply. We do not use your number for marketing.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet the temple's record-keeping needs. You may request deletion of your data (see Contact below).

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
